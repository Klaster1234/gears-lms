# GEARS LMS — Botanical Papercut Art Direction

## Concept

**Style:** Layered paper cut art (kirigami/wycinanka) in monochromatic forest greens on ivory paper.
Each illustration looks like it's built from 4-6 layers of hand-cut paper in different shades of green, with soft shadows between layers creating depth. Botanical elements (leaves, roots, seeds) weave through every composition. The feel is: European craft meets modern editorial design.

**Color palette for all images:**
- Darkest layer: `#022C22` (near-black green)
- Primary layer: `#064E3B` (forest green)
- Mid layer: `#047857` (medium green)
- Accent layer: `#0D9488` (teal)
- Highlight layer: `#34D399` (mint green)
- Background/light: `#FAF8F0` (warm ivory/cream)
- Soft fill: `#84A98C` (sage)
